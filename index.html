About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate majoring computer vision with deep learning at Computer Vision Lab in Seoul National University, under professor Kyoung Mu Lee. Early in my PhD studies, I was interested in low-level vision problems such as image restoration and frame interpolation. I have served as a co-organizer on two tracks(image deblurring and video super-resolution) at NTIRE Workshop in CVPR 2021.
Recently, I have been interested in 3D vision problems, and have been researching 3D scene reconstruction using 3D Gaussian splatting, and further, high-quality and large-scale scene generation problems. In particular, I am working on holistic scene reconstruction by utilizing wide FoV of omnidirectional images such as ODGS and OmniSplat.
